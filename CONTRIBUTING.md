# Contributing to Protocol Signal Lab

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Protocol Signal Lab.

This project aims to monitor and analyze signals across crypto infrastructure ecosystems such as governance activity, developer updates, and on-chain developments.

---

## How You Can Contribute

Contributions may include:

- Identifying new signal sources
- Tracking governance proposals across protocols
- Monitoring developer activity on infrastructure projects
- Documenting ecosystem developments
- Improving signal analysis and reporting

---

## Adding New Signal Sources

If you discover useful sources for monitoring ecosystem activity:

1. Add the source to `sources.md`
2. Briefly describe why it is important
3. Ensure the source is relevant to crypto infrastructure development

---

## Logging New Signals

When you observe an important ecosystem signal:

1. Add a new entry in `signals-log.md`
2. Include:
   - Date
   - Source
   - Type of signal
   - Observation
   - Potential impact

---

## Creating Research Reports

When several signals point to meaningful developments:

1. Create a new report inside the `reports/` directory
2. Use a structured format similar to previous reports
3. Focus on clear insights rather than speculation

---

## Contribution Philosophy

Protocol Signal Lab focuses on:

- Signal detection rather than hype
- Infrastructure developments rather than short-term market noise
- Clear research insights rather than speculation

---

## Future Contributions

Future development may include:

- Automated signal collection
- AI-based signal analysis
- Real-time infrastructure monitoring dashboards

All thoughtful contributions are welcome.
